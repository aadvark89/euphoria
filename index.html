Methodology

How the analysis works

01

Garman-Klass estimator

Uses high, low, open, and close for each hour — far more accurate than close-to-close returns. Captures intraday swings that directly impact short-duration tap bets.

02

Session-layer overlay

Three global sessions: Asia (00–08), Europe (07–16), US (13–22 UTC). Overlap periods spike both volume and vol. Risk scores weight session overlap explicitly.

03

Funding rate pressure

Perp futures settle funding at 00:00, 08:00, 16:00 UTC. Pre-settlement position adjustments create mini-squeezes — elevated directional risk in those hours.

04

Composite scoring

Final score = vol risk (40%) + session activity (30%) + macro timing (15%) + funding pressure (15%). Below 35 = safe for straight-line bets. Above 65 = avoid.
